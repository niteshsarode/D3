Analysis

Chart 1 is the scatter plot that plots the players who played in AUS Open Finals for 10 years from 2004 to 2014. Clicking upon the dots in scatter plot we will get a bar chart for the Average First Serve speed and Average Second Serve speed of both the players for that match. From these graphs we can compare their speed and deduce the fastest serve speed player between them for the match.
