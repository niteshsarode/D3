Analysis

Above on the left we have a map interactive visualization for all the countries which shows two types of data channels (choropleth & bubble). The map has 3 states in which you can view information (show population data in choropleth, show population density with choropleth and show population density with bubble size).
For population shown with choropleth the more dark the shade of blue the more population the country has.
For population density with choropleth the more dark the shade of brown the more population density the country has.
For population density with bubbles, larger bubbles correspond to larger population density for the country.

The data map provides two interactions, hover and mouse click.
When hovering you can see the Population Density or Population of the hovered country.
When you click on a country the detail view opens up below the datamap which shows the pie chart stating the distribution of GDP across three fields Agriculture, Industry and Service sector. When a country is clicked the selected country is highlighted in the datamap and its corresponding stats are provided in the detailed view.
